# Northwind Robotics — Employee Handbook (Sample)

This is a sample document so you can test the agent's `search_documents` tool.
The facts below are fictional and intentionally specific, so you can tell when
the agent is answering from this file versus from general knowledge.

## Time Off

Full-time employees at Northwind Robotics receive **24 days** of paid annual
leave per year, plus **6 company wellness days** that cannot be carried over.
Annual leave begins accruing on the employee's first day and may be used after
the 90-day probation period.

## Remote Work

Northwind operates on a **hybrid schedule**: employees work from the office on
Tuesdays and Wednesdays, and may work remotely the rest of the week. Fully
remote arrangements require director approval and are reviewed every 6 months.

## Equipment

Every engineer is issued a **laptop, a 4K monitor, and a USD 500 annual
peripherals budget**. Hardware requests over USD 500 must be approved by the
employee's manager and the IT lead, Priya Raman.

## Offices

Northwind has three offices: the headquarters in **Portland, Oregon**, an
engineering hub in **Tallinn, Estonia**, and a sales office in **Singapore**.
The Portland office is open from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekdays.

## Expense Reimbursement

Expenses must be submitted within **30 days** through the Atlas portal. Meals
during business travel are reimbursed up to **USD 75 per day**. Receipts are
required for any expense over USD 25.

## Support Contacts

For IT issues, contact the help desk at extension 4500. For HR questions,
email people@northwind.example. The office manager in Portland is Dale Whitman.
